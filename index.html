MIT CSAIL, Cambridge MA · giovanni _at_ csail.mit.edu

Short Bio

I am a postdoctoral associate at MIT CSAIL. I received the PhD degree in Computer Science from the University of Modena in 2016. My doctoral dissertation won the PhD Thesis Award from the IEEE Computer Society Italy Section. My research interests include data integration and big data management.
